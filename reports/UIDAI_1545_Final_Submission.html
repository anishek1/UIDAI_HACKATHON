üí° Our Innovation: 5 Engineered Metrics

Metric Formula Question Answered
IFI - Identity Freshness Index (Demo + Bio Updates) / Enrolments How actively is data being refreshed?
CLCR - Child Lifecycle Capture Rate Bio Updates (5-17) / Expected Are children getting mandatory updates?
TAES - Temporal Access Equity Weekend Avg / Weekday Avg Is weekend access equitable?
UCR - Update Completeness Ratio Active Districts / Total Districts How complete is geographic coverage?
AAUP - Age-Adjusted Update Propensity Per-capita rate vs National Who over/under-updates?
